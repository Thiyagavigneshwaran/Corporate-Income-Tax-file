What is Tax? & Why we are Paying Tax?

Tax is a mandatory financial contribution imposed by a government on individuals, businesses, and other entities. It is used to fund public services and infrastructure like: Roads and transportation,Education and schools, Hospitals and healthcare,National defense and police,Social welfare programs,Government operations.

We pay taxes for the functioning and development of our society. Here's why it's important: ✅ Public Infrastructure Roads, railways, airports, bridges, etc., are all built and maintained using tax money. ✅ Education & Healthcare Government schools, colleges, hospitals, and health schemes are funded through taxes. ✅ Security & Law Enforcement Salaries of police, military, and judiciary come from taxes. ✅ Welfare Programs Subsidies, pensions, rural employment schemes, and food programs use tax funding. ✅ National Development Large-scale projects, technology research, and poverty eradication rely on tax income.
